> Build a daily automated intelligence pipeline for the Moody's specialist agent.
>
> What it does:
> Every day at 6:00 AM ET, run a scheduled job that collects intelligence across 5 categories and saves a structured brief to the vault. Additionally, every Sunday at 7:00 AM ET, generate a weekly strategic digest.
>
> 5 Intelligence Feeds:
>
> 1. Moody's Corporate News — Search site:moodys.com + news_search("Moody's") for any new press releases, product announcements, leadership changes, earnings, or strategic moves. Focus on anything related to Banking Solutions, Lending, KYC, Risk Analytics, or data products.
>
> 2. Banking Segment Specifics — Search for news about Moody's banking products: Credit Lens, Moody's Analytics banking, OnlineALM, Orbis, BvD. Include any customer wins, partnerships, or product launches in the banking vertical.
>
> 3. Competitor Intelligence — Search for latest news from ALL of these competitors and partners:
>
>    Credit Rating & Data Peers:
>    - Bloomberg — Bloomberg Data, Enterprise Data, AI initiatives
>    - S&P Global — Market Intelligence, Capital IQ, data strategy
>    - Fitch — Fitch Ratings, Fitch Solutions, banking analytics, data products
>    - Nasdaq — Nasdaq Financial Technology, AxiomSL, Calypso, risk/regulatory tech
>
>    Banking Tech / Lending Platforms:
>    - nCino — Bank operating system, lending automation, AI in banking workflows
>    - QRM — Credit risk, ALM, FTP, balance sheet management (direct Credit Lens competitor)
>    - Empyrean (Emperion) — Lending technology, credit decisioning, banking analytics
>
>    Data & AI Infrastructure:
>    - Quantexa — Entity resolution, knowledge graph, agentic AI, banking deals
>    - Databricks — Financial services, lakehouse for banking, Delta Sharing
>
>    Regulatory & Compliance Partners:
>    - Regnology — Regulatory reporting tech
>    - FinregE — Regulatory intelligence automation
>    - ValidMind — Model risk management, AI governance
>
> 4. Enterprise AI Trends — Search for:
>    - Agentic AI in banking and financial services
>    - Enterprise LLM deployments in regulated industries
>    - AI governance and regulation updates (EU AI Act, US banking regulators)
>    - MCP (Model Context Protocol) enterprise adoption
>    - CDM / data standards in financial services
>
> 5. Industry Analyst Coverage — Scrape/search these analyst and research sites for relevant reports, rankings, and commentary:
>    - Celent (celent.com) — Banking technology research, vendor rankings, IT spending reports. Search for any mentions of Moody's, Credit Lens, lending tech, risk analytics, ALM, banking AI, and any competitor named above.
>    - Chartis Research (chartis-research.com) — Risk technology rankings (RiskTech100, quadrants), credit risk, market risk, model risk, RegTech. Search for any vendor rankings, quadrant reports, or category analyses that mention Moody's or competitors.
>    - Also search for reports from Forrester, Gartner, and IDC covering banking technology, risk analytics, or enterprise AI in financial services.

> Implementation details:
> - Use the existing web_search and news_search tools — no new APIs needed
> - For Celent and Chartis, use web_search with site:celent.com and site:chartis-research.com queries
> - Register both as scheduled reports in the alerts/briefs system alongside existing Daily News and Market Summary jobs
> - The Moody's agent (moodys) should automatically read the latest daily brief AND the latest weekly digest when invoked for any Moody's-related task — add both to the agent's context loading sequence
> - Keep the last 7 days of daily briefs and last 4 weekly digests; archive older ones to Archive/Moody's Intelligence/
> - If a search returns no new results for a category, note "No new developments" rather than omitting the section
> - Tag each item with a relevance indicator: 🔴 High (directly impacts Moody's Banking), 🟡 Medium (industry trend worth watching), 🟢 Low (background context)
